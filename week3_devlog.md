# Week 3 Development Log

## Project: Augmented Reality Smart Guide

### Major Improvement This Week

In the third week, we focused on solving the biggest drawback ---
explaining how the system works in real usage.

### Refined Working Model

We structured the working process clearly:

1.  When users enter a monument or museum, they open the app.
2.  The app assists them by suggesting where to begin their exploration.
3.  When users point their camera at a monument or artifact, the system
    identifies it.
4.  The app displays:
    -   Historical background
    -   Importance and uniqueness
    -   Fun facts
    -   Special features
5.  Information is shown in an interactive and engaging AR format.

### What Changed

Instead of a vague AR/VR tour guide concept, we now have: - A clear
workflow - A defined user interaction model - A structured explanation
of how the system operates

### Learning Outcome

We learned that: - Research strengthens project credibility. - Clear
user-flow explanation is essential. - A project must be presented both
conceptually and practically.
