Week 3 Development Log

Project: Augmented Reality Smart Guide

Major Improvement This Week

In the third week, we focused on solving the biggest drawback — explaining how the system works in real usage.

Refined Working Model

We structured the working process clearly:

  1. When users enter a monument or museum, they open the app.
  2. The app assists them by suggesting where to begin their exploration.
  3. When users point their camera at a monument or artifact, the system identifies it.
  4. The app displays:
    • Historical background
    • Importance and uniqueness
    • Fun facts
    • Special features
  5. Information is shown in an interactive and engaging AR format.

What Changed

  • A clear workflow
  • A defined user interaction model
  • A structured explanation of how the system operates

Learning Outcome

  • Research strengthens project credibility.
  • Clear user-flow explanation is essential.
  • A project must be presented both conceptually and practically.

Next Step

  • Begin Unity-based prototype
  • Design UI layout
  • Implement image recognition for AR overlay
