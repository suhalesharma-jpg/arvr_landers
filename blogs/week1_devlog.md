# Week 1 Development Log

## Project: Augmented Reality Smart Guide

### Idea Presented

In the first week, we presented a basic and somewhat unclear idea of
creating a Tour Guide system using AR/VR technology. Our concept was to
help visitors explore monuments and museums interactively.

### What Went Well

We were able to explain the *need* for such a system properly. We
discussed: - Tour guides are not always available. - Many visitors do
not understand the importance of monuments. - Traditional audio guides
are often boring and lengthy.

### Major Drawback

Although we explained the need clearly, we were not able to explain the
**actual purpose** of the project properly.

Our idea appeared: - Too broad (AR/VR tour guide without clear focus) -
Not clearly differentiated from existing systems - Lacking a defined
objective

### Feedback Received

The teacher suggested: - Clearly define the core purpose. - Specify what
problem we are solving. - Avoid presenting a vague AR/VR concept.

### Reflection

We realized that having a "good idea" is not enough --- we must clearly
define: - The purpose - The problem - The impact
