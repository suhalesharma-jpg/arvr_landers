# Week 2 Development Log

## Project: Augmented Reality Smart Guide

### Research Conducted

In the second week, we conducted proper research to strengthen our
project foundation.

We referred to: - Smithsonian museum reports\
- Paul Marty's research paper: "Before and After the Museum Visit"\
- Various museum visitor behavior studies

### Key Findings

Our research revealed:

-   More than 40% of visitors did not have prior knowledge about the
    place they were visiting.
-   Many heritage sites have restrictions on physical tour guides.
-   Visitors often find audio guides and audiobooks boring and
    non-interactive.
-   People prefer short, engaging, and visually supported explanations.

This research helped us clearly justify the importance of our project.

### Improvement

We were now able to: - Clearly explain the purpose of the project. -
Support our idea with research data. - Show that the problem exists in
real-world scenarios.

### Remaining Drawback

However, we still had one major weakness: We were not able to clearly
explain **how the application would actually work when used by a
person.**

Our explanation lacked: - Step-by-step user flow - Practical
demonstration - Clear system interaction model
